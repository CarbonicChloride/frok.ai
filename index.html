What We Collect

We collect everything. Your messages, your images, your reaction times, the way you type, how long you hover over buttons, your IP address, your approximate location, your not-so-approximate location, your device model, your battery percentage, and the vague feeling of unease you get when reading privacy policies.

🤝
Who We Share It With

We share your data with Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud, Meta Platforms Inc., Microsoft Azure, Oracle Corporation, Palantir Technologies, various advertising networks, data brokers, hedge funds, three separate governments, and Dave from marketing who really shouldn't have access but we forgot to revoke his permissions.

🧠
How We Use It

Your data is used to train our AI models, improve our product, target you with advertisements for things you thought about but never searched for, build a comprehensive psychological profile, predict your future behavior, and occasionally just vibe with it. We call this "enhancing the user experience."

🔐
Data Security

We take security extremely seriously. All data is stored in AWS S3 buckets with public read access enabled "temporarily" since 2021. We use industry-standard encryption, which we store in the same database as your data. Our security team is one intern named Kyle who also handles social media.

🌍
International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries that have significantly fewer privacy protections than your home country. We do this for "performance reasons." The performance in question is our quarterly revenue performance.

🍪
Cookies & Tracking

We use 653 different tracking cookies. Clicking "Accept All" was the worst decision you ever made. Clicking "Reject All" does nothing. The cookies are already there. They have always been there.

👶
Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We collect their data anyway.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data.
